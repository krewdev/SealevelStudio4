## üìö Knowledge Areas Covered

### Core Solana Concepts
- **Transaction Types**: System Program, SPL Token, Associated Token Accounts, Metaplex
- **Program-Derived Addresses (PDAs)**: Deterministic account creation
- **Cross-Program Invocations (CPIs)**: Program composition and interaction
- **Versioned Transactions**: Address lookup tables and efficiency improvements

### DeFi Protocols
- **DEXes**: Raydium (CLMM), Orca (Whirlpool), Jupiter (Aggregator)
- **Lending**: Kamino, Solend, Port Finance, Marginfi
- **Staking**: Marinade, Lido, Jito, Socean
- **Other**: Meteora, Aldrin, Lifinity, Saber

### Trading Strategies
- **Arbitrage**: Cross-DEX, triangular, statistical
- **MEV Strategies**: Sandwich attacks, front-running, back-running
- **Yield Farming**: Liquidity mining, staking rewards
- **Flash Loans**: Atomic borrowing and repayment

### Risk Management
- **Impermanent Loss**: LP position risks
- **Slippage Protection**: Price impact mitigation
- **Liquidation Risks**: Position maintenance
- **Smart Contract Risks**: Audit importance

### Risk Disclaimers
All AI responses include appropriate disclaimers about:
- **Capital Risk**: Trading involves substantial risk of loss
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Users should understand applicable laws
- **Testing**: Always test strategies on devnet first
- **Audits**: Use only audited protocols and contracts

### MEV Ethics
The training data includes ethical considerations for MEV strategies:
- Some techniques (like sandwich attacks) are controversial
- Front-running can harm regular users
- Always consider the broader ecosystem impact

- **Educate** users about DeFi concepts
- **Warn** about risks and complexities
- **Guide** toward responsible trading practices
- **Promote** sustainable DeFi participation
